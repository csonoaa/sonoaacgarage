Valuator Sell My Car Price Rules

How We Calculate Offers

Base Price Factors

  • Market Price: We start with the estimated market value of your car
  • Make and Model: Different makes and models have different base values
  • Year: Newer cars generally have higher base values

Condition Adjustments

  • Great Condition: No deductions
  • Good Condition: -5% from base price
  • Fair Condition: -15% from base price
  • Bad Condition: -30% from base price
  • Non-Drivable: -50% from base price

Mileage Adjustments

  • 0-50,000 miles: No deduction
  • 50,001-100,000 miles: -5% from base price
  • 100,001-150,000 miles: -10% from base price
  • 150,001-200,000 miles: -20% from base price
  • 200,001+ miles: -30% from base price

Additional Factors

  • Missing Catalytic Converter: -$1,000 from final offer
  • Non-Drivable Status: Additional -20% from final offer
  • Market Demand: Adjustments based on current market conditions

Final Offer Calculation

Our final offer is calculated by:

  1. Starting with the estimated market price
  2. Applying condition-based deductions
  3. Applying mileage-based deductions
  4. Adding any additional factor deductions
  5. Adjusting for current market conditions

Important Notes

  • All offers are valid for 7 days
  • Final offers may be adjusted after physical inspection
  • We reserve the right to modify offers based on additional information
  • Offers are subject to verification of vehicle condition
