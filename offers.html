Our Pricing Rules

How We Calculate Your Offer

We use a transparent pricing system based on market data and vehicle condition. Here's how it works:

Base Price

We start with our base price for your specific make and model, determined by current market data.

Year Adjustment

We apply a 2% depreciation per year from the current year.

Condition Factors

  • Great: No adjustment
  • Good: -5%
  • Fair: -15%
  • Bad: -30%
  • Non-Drivable: -50%

Mileage Adjustments

  • 0-50,000 miles: No adjustment
  • 50,001-100,000 miles: -5%
  • 100,001-150,000 miles: -10%
  • 150,001-200,000 miles: -20%
  • 200,000+ miles: -30%

Additional Factors

  • Missing Catalytic Converter: -$1,000
  • Non-Drivable: -20%
  • High-Value States (CA, NY, TX, FL): +5%
