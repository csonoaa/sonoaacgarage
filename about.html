About sonoaacgarage

Your trusted partner in selling cars quickly and fairly

Who We Are

sonoaacgarage helps people sell their cars faster by providing fair, transparent offers based on real-world market conditions. We believe in making the car selling process simple, quick, and stress-free.

How Offers Work

  • Base Price: We start with the current market value of your car
  • Company Cut: We apply a 37% deduction to cover our services
  • Mileage Adjustments:
    • Under 100,000 miles: No deduction
    • 100,000-150,000 miles: -5%
    • Over 150,000 miles: -10%
  • Condition Adjustments:
    • Great: No deduction
    • Good: -5%
    • Fair: -10%
    • Bad: -15%
    • Non-Drivable: Maximum offer of $900
  • Additional Deductions:
    • Missing catalytic converter: -10%
