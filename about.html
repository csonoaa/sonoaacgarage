About SonoAAC Garage

Our Story

SonoAAC Garage was founded with a simple mission: to make selling your car easy, fair, and transparent. We understand that selling a car can be stressful, which is why we've created a streamlined process that puts you in control.

Our Process

1. Get Your Offer

Use our online valuator to get an instant offer based on your car's details.

2. Schedule Inspection

Visit our location for a quick inspection to verify your car's condition.

3. Get Paid

Complete the paperwork and receive payment on the spot.
